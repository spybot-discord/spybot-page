One Bot, One Community

Verification

You can easily set CAPTCHA verification, users will get CAPTCHA message to verify themselves and Spy Bot will want them to enter the code they got in order to be verified.

Whitelist

There are some people that should not be affected by the logging system, you can easily add them to the whitelist where they are not affected.

Logging System

Some actions are best to keep track of, like creation of channels, kicks, removal of roles, etc..., Spy Bot has a logging system that will log every action like this and will punish the user once it gets to a configured limit.

Suggestions

Users may want to help you grow your server and give you awesome ideas!, that's where Spy Bot's suggestions come in. They allow you to send a suggestion to a certain channel and people can upvote it or downvote it.

Quarantine

Users/Bots may be trying to raid your servers, that's where quarantine comes in, it will prevent users from doing anything that may cause harm to your server.

Moderation

Act fast! If a user is being harmful to your server, you can use commands such as kick or ban to moderate
